Why choose Heavy Cuddly for conversion intelligence?

We help teams understand what happens after the click. Landing page behavior, funnel friction, and quality signals are reviewed together to find stronger conversion decisions.

  • Identify friction points across landing pages and user journeys.

What this service covers

Conversion intelligence helps teams understand what happens between attention and action. We review landing pages, user behavior, analytics signals, offer clarity, and funnel friction to identify which changes are most likely to improve outcomes.

Best fit when you need to

  • Understand why traffic is not turning into useful leads or sales.
  • Find landing page friction before redesigning everything.
  • Prioritize conversion tests with clearer evidence.
  • Connect campaign quality with on-site behavior and outcomes.

Conversion FAQ

Is this the same as analytics reporting?

No. Reporting shows what happened. Conversion intelligence focuses on what should change next.

Do we need lots of traffic?

More data helps, but qualitative friction and structural issues can often be found before traffic is large.

Can this support paid campaigns?

Yes. It helps campaign teams understand whether clicks are turning into useful business outcomes.
