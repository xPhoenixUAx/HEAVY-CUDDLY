FAQ

Questions before choosing a service?

Short answers for choosing the right starting point and understanding how Heavy Cuddly structures the work.

Which service should we start with?

Start where the constraint is sharpest: unclear direction, weak acquisition, slow creative testing, messy operations, or conversion friction.

Can several services work together?

Yes. The services are designed to connect, so strategy can guide media, creative, reporting, and conversion improvements inside one workflow.

Do you support existing marketing teams?

Yes. We can support internal teams with audits, campaign systems, reporting routines, process structure, or focused execution support.

Are prices fixed for every project?

No. Scope, channels, timeline, and implementation depth define the final cost. A manager can confirm the exact estimate after reviewing your needs.

What happens after we contact you?

We review your current setup, identify the useful starting point, and suggest a focused next step instead of forcing a broad package.
