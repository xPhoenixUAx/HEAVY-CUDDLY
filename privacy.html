Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how HEAVY CUDDLY s.r.o. collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use heavycuddly.com.

Effective date: 1 January 2026

Last updated: 1 January 2026

01. Who We Are

This Website is operated by HEAVY CUDDLY s.r.o., a digital marketing agency providing strategy, advertising, and performance-focused marketing services to clients across Europe and internationally.

For the purposes of the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR"), HEAVY CUDDLY s.r.o. acts as the data controller in respect of personal data collected through this Website.

We are committed to handling personal data responsibly, transparently, and in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

02. Data We Collect

We may collect personal data when you interact with our Website, contact us, or engage our services.

  • Identity data: name, surname, company name, job title.
  • Contact data: email address, phone number, business address.
  • Financial data: billing details, VAT number, invoicing information where applicable.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, time zone.
  • Usage data: pages visited, session duration, navigation paths, interaction data.
  • Marketing data: communication preferences and campaign engagement.
  • Business data: information related to services, including marketing goals, budgets, and project scope.

We do not intentionally collect special categories of personal data. If such data is provided, it will be handled appropriately or deleted.

03. How We Use Your Data

We use personal data for the following purposes:

  • responding to inquiries and requests;
  • providing, managing, and improving our services;
  • maintaining business relationships;
  • processing invoices and payments;
  • analysing Website performance and user behaviour;
  • supporting marketing and advertising activities;
  • ensuring security and preventing misuse;
  • complying with legal and regulatory obligations.

We do not process personal data for purposes incompatible with these objectives.

04. Legal Basis for Processing

We rely on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance: where processing is necessary to deliver services.
  • Legitimate interests: to operate and improve our business and Website.
  • Legal obligation: to comply with applicable laws.
  • Consent: for marketing communications and cookies where required.

You may withdraw your consent at any time.

05. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary:

  • client and financial data: up to 7 years;
  • inquiry data: up to 2 years;
  • analytics data: up to 26 months;
  • marketing data: until consent withdrawal.

Where appropriate, data may be anonymised for analytical purposes.

06. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell personal data.

We may share data with:

  • service providers, including hosting, CRM systems, and email tools;
  • advertising platforms, such as Google and Meta;
  • analytics providers;
  • professional advisers, including legal and accounting advisers;
  • public authorities where required by law.

All third parties are required to process data securely and in accordance with applicable laws.

07. International Data Transfers

Some service providers may process data outside the European Economic Area (EEA).

Where this occurs, we ensure appropriate safeguards, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or other approved transfer mechanisms.

08. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • access your personal data;
  • correct inaccurate data;
  • request deletion;
  • restrict processing;
  • receive your data through data portability;
  • object to processing;
  • withdraw consent.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

09. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to ensure proper Website functionality, analyse usage and performance, and support advertising and remarketing.

For detailed information, please refer to our Cookie Policy.
