Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how HEAVY CUDDLY s.r.o. uses cookies and similar technologies on heavycuddly.com.

Effective date: 1 January 2026

01. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website.

They may be:

  • session cookies;
  • persistent cookies;
  • first-party or third-party cookies.

We may also use technologies such as pixels, scripts, and local storage.

02. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • ensure Website functionality;
  • remember user preferences;
  • analyse traffic and behaviour;
  • measure advertising effectiveness;
  • support remarketing campaigns.

03. Types of Cookies

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: required for the Website to function properly.
  • Analytics Cookies: used to understand user behaviour and improve performance.
  • Marketing Cookies: used for advertising, tracking, and audience building.

05. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services including:

  • Google;

These providers may process data according to their own privacy policies.

06. Managing Cookies

Users can manage cookies via:

  • cookie consent banner;
  • browser settings;
  • opt-out tools.

07. Updates
